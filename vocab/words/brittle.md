# brittle

**Part of Speech:** adjective

**Definition:** Hard but easily broken; fragile under stress


**Examples:**
1. The brittle branches snapped under the weight of the snow.
2. A brittle world model works perfectly on simple cases but shatters on edge cases.
3. The old photograph was yellowed and brittle.
4. Her brittle smile suggested she was hiding deep frustration.
5. Brittle materials like glass break without bending first.
6. The ceasefire proved brittle and collapsed within weeks.
7. His brittle confidence evaporated the moment he faced real criticism.
8. Brittle bones are a common health concern for the elderly.
9. The negotiations were brittle, falling apart at the first sign of disagreement.
10. She spoke in a brittle tone that masked her anxiety.
